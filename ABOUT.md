## What Is Cosmos ERP?

Cosmos ERP is a cloud-based, multi-tenant Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) platform designed specifically for Nigerian businesses. It is an all-in-one business management system that combines accounting, invoicing, inventory, sales, human resources, payroll, e-commerce, and customer service into a single unified platform — with deep integration into Nigeria's tax and regulatory infrastructure.

The platform is built and operated by **Roland Consult** as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) product. Each business that signs up becomes a "tenant" on the platform with its own fully isolated workspace — their data, users, settings, and workflows are completely separate from every other business on the system.

## What Problem Does It Solve?

Nigerian businesses face a unique set of challenges that global software tools don't address:

### 1. Tax Compliance Is Hard
Nigeria's FIRS now requires businesses to submit electronic invoices through the NRS system. Each invoice must receive an IRN (Invoice Reference Number) and a QR code stamp before it is considered valid. Most accounting software used in Nigeria — QuickBooks, Wave, Zoho — has zero NRS integration. Business owners are forced to manually log into government portals, re-enter invoice data, and download stamps. Cosmos ERP does this automatically.

### 2. Payroll Is Complex
Paying staff in Nigeria isn't just about transferring salaries. Every payroll run requires calculating:
- **PAYE (Pay As You Earn)** — Income tax using Nigeria's graduated tax bands, after applying the Consolidated Relief Allowance (CRA).
- **Pension** — 8% from the employee and 10% from the employer, as mandated by the Pension Reform Act 2014.
- **NHF (National Housing Fund)** — 2.5% of basic salary.
- **ITF (Industrial Training Fund)** — 1% of total annual payroll.

After calculation, businesses need to generate a **NIBSS-format bank payment file** to submit to their bank for bulk salary transfer. No mainstream international ERP handles any of this natively. Cosmos ERP computes everything automatically and generates the bank file.

### 3. Too Many Disconnected Tools
A typical Nigerian SME might use one app for invoicing, another for inventory, a spreadsheet for payroll, WhatsApp for customer communication, and yet another tool for their online store. Data is scattered across systems. Reconciliation takes hours. Mistakes are common. Cosmos ERP puts everything in one place — when a sale is made, the invoice is created, inventory is updated, the accounting journal entry is posted, the VAT is calculated, and the NRS submission is triggered — all in a single flow.

### 4. No Affordable Nigerian ERP Exists
Enterprise systems like SAP and Oracle cost millions of naira to deploy and require expensive Nigerian customization. Local alternatives are often outdated, lack modern UIs, or don't support multi-tenancy. Cosmos ERP offers a modern, beautiful interface at subscription prices starting from ₦15,000/month.

## What Can You Do With Cosmos ERP?

### Finance & Accounting
- Maintain a full **Chart of Accounts** (Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Revenue, Expenses) with hierarchical parent-child structure.
- Create **journal entries** with double-entry bookkeeping (every debit has a matching credit).
- Track **multiple currencies** — set your base currency (NGN), add USD, GBP, EUR with exchange rates.
- Manage **tax filings** — track your VAT, WHT, PAYE, and CIT obligations by period.
- Generate financial reports: **Trial Balance**, **Profit & Loss**, **Balance Sheet**, **Cash Flow Statement**, **Aged Receivables/Payables**.

### Sales & Invoicing
- Create professional **quotations** and send them to customers. When accepted, convert a quote into an invoice with one click.
- Issue **invoices** with line items, VAT (7.5%), WHT deductions, discounts, payment terms, and multi-currency support.
- Submit invoices to **NRS/FIRS** automatically and receive the IRN number and QR code stamp. Store the UBL/XML version for audit purposes.
- Track **payments** — record full and partial payments. Integrate with **Paystack** for online payment collection.
- Manage customer **credit limits** — set how much each customer can owe you before the system blocks further invoicing.
- Send invoices via **email** or **WhatsApp** directly from the system.

### Inventory & Warehousing
- Manage your **product catalog** with SKU codes, barcodes, cost prices, selling prices, VAT/WHT rates, images, weight, and dimensions.
- Run **multiple warehouses** — track stock levels per product per warehouse.
- See a full **audit trail** of every stock movement: purchases received, sales delivered, warehouse transfers, manual adjustments, returns, and damages.
- Calculate **landed costs** for imported goods — allocate freight, customs duty, insurance, and handling charges to product cost basis.
- Create **purchase orders** to your suppliers with line items, receiving, and partial delivery tracking.
- Set **reorder points** and quantities — the system alerts you when stock drops below threshold.

### Human Resources & Payroll
- Maintain a complete **employee directory** with staff ID, NIN, BVN, date of birth, department, job title, employment type, bank details, pension PIN, NHF number, and tax ID.
- Run **monthly payroll** — the system automatically calculates gross salary, basic salary, housing allowance, transport allowance, and all statutory deductions:
  - PAYE using Nigeria's graduated tax bands
  - Employee pension (8%) and employer pension (10%)
  - NHF (2.5% of basic)
  - ITF (1% of total payroll)
- Generate detailed **payslips** for each employee showing every line item.
- Export a **NIBSS bank payment file** for bulk salary disbursement through your bank.

### E-Commerce Marketplace
- Any business on Cosmos ERP can toggle their products onto a **shared public marketplace** — a B2B/B2C online store where buyers can browse, search, and purchase.
- Buyers complete checkout via **Paystack** — the payment goes into **escrow** (held by the platform).
- When the buyer confirms delivery, the funds are **released to the seller** minus the platform's commission (default 5%).
- The marketplace supports **product categories**, **search**, **featured listings**, **ratings and reviews**, **shipping cost calculation**, **logistics tracking**, and **order lifecycle management** (pending → confirmed → processing → shipped → delivered).
- Sellers manage their marketplace listings from within their ERP dashboard — no separate seller portal needed.

### Customer Care & Call Center
- Create **support tickets** for customer issues, categorized by channel (Email, Phone, WhatsApp, Walk-in, Web Form), category (Billing, Technical, Delivery, Complaint, Refund, General), and priority (Low, Medium, High, Urgent).
- Track ticket lifecycle: **Open → In Progress → Waiting on Customer → Resolved → Closed**.
- **Assign tickets** to specific team members. Agents see their personal queue.
- Add **conversation threads** to each ticket — both customer-facing replies and internal notes visible only to your team.
- Log **phone calls** — track inbound and outbound calls with customer name, phone number, duration, outcome (Connected, No Answer, Voicemail, Busy, Failed), and notes.
- Link calls to existing tickets for a complete interaction history.
- Set **SLA deadlines** and monitor overdue tickets.
- A dedicated **Agent Portal** gives customer care staff a streamlined, focused interface — they log in at `/agent-login` and see only their dashboard, tickets, and call logs without the full ERP complexity.

### Point of Sale (POS)
- A built-in **POS interface** for retail and walk-in sales.
- Quick product search, barcode scanning support, cart management.
- Process payments and generate receipts.
- Sales automatically update inventory and accounting.

## The Four Applications

Cosmos ERP is not a single application — it is a **platform** made up of four interconnected applications that share a single backend API and database:

### 1. The ERP Application (Port 3060)
This is the main business application. Business owners, accountants, sales staff, warehouse managers, and HR personnel log in here to manage their day-to-day operations. It contains 22 pages covering every module listed above. There are 8 user roles (Owner, Admin, Accountant, Sales, Warehouse, HR, Staff, Viewer) that control what each person can see and do.

### 2. The Marketplace (Port 5174)
This is the public-facing e-commerce storefront. Anyone can browse products listed by Cosmos ERP tenants, add items to their cart, and check out using Paystack. It has 6 pages: Home, Listings, Product Detail, Cart, Checkout, and Order Confirmation.

### 3. The Admin Portal (Port 5175)
This is Roland Consult's internal super-admin dashboard. Only Roland Consult staff use this to manage the entire platform — reviewing tenant KYC documents (TIN, CAC registration), monitoring the NRS API bridge health, moderating marketplace listings, viewing platform analytics, managing logistics, and reviewing audit logs. It has 9 pages and 4 admin roles (Super Admin, Finance Admin, Support, Compliance).

### 4. The Agent Portal (Embedded in ERP at /agent)
This is a streamlined version of the ERP designed specifically for customer care agents. Agents log in at `/agent-login` and access only their support dashboard, ticket queue, and call log — without seeing the full ERP modules. It shares the same backend and authentication system but presents a focused, green-themed interface.

## How Multi-Tenancy Works

Every business on Cosmos ERP is called a **tenant**. When a new business registers:

1. They fill in their business details — name, trading name, TIN, RC number, address, contact info, industry.
2. Their account starts in **KYC Pending** status. A Roland Consult admin reviews their TIN and CAC documents.
3. Once **approved**, the tenant gets full access. They start on a **14-day free trial**.
4. After the trial, they choose a subscription plan: **Starter** (₦15,000/mo), **Growth** (₦50,000/mo), or **Enterprise** (₦150,000/mo).

All tenant data is stored in the same database but **completely isolated** using a `tenantId` column on every table. When User A from Company A logs in, they can only ever see Company A's data. The system enforces this at the middleware level — before any database query runs, the tenant filter is applied automatically.

## How Nigerian Tax Compliance Works

### NRS E-Invoicing Flow
1. A user creates an invoice in the ERP.
2. When the invoice is finalized, the system converts it to **UBL 2.1 XML format** (the international PEPPOL standard that FIRS adopted).
3. The XML is submitted to the **NRS API** (FIRS's electronic invoicing system).
4. NRS validates the invoice and returns an **IRN** (Invoice Reference Number) and a **QR code**.
5. The IRN and QR code are stored on the invoice and displayed on the PDF.
6. The entire API exchange (request, response, duration, errors) is logged in the `NRSLog` table for audit purposes.
7. Roland Consult admins can monitor NRS bridge health — success rates, failures, pending queue — from the Admin Portal.

### Payroll Tax Flow
1. HR selects a month and runs payroll.
2. For each employee, the system calculates:
   - **Gross Salary** = Basic + Housing + Transport + Other Allowances
   - **CRA (Consolidated Relief Allowance)** = ₦200,000 or 1% of gross (whichever is higher) + 20% of gross
   - **Taxable Income** = Gross − CRA − Pension − NHF
   - **PAYE** = Applied to taxable income using graduated bands (First ₦300K at 7%, next ₦300K at 11%, next ₦500K at 15%, next ₦500K at 19%, next ₦1.6M at 21%, above ₦3.2M at 24%)
   - **Pension** = Employee 8%, Employer 10%
   - **NHF** = 2.5% of basic salary
   - **Net Pay** = Gross − PAYE − Employee Pension − NHF − Other Deductions
3. Individual payslips are generated.
4. A NIBSS-format file is created for bulk bank transfer.

## Security & Data Protection

- **Row-level data isolation** — Every database query is filtered by `tenantId`. No business can ever see another business's data.
- **JWT authentication** — Short-lived access tokens (15 minutes) with refresh token rotation.
- **Password hashing** — bcrypt with salting, never stored in plain text.
- **Role-based access control (RBAC)** — 8 tenant-level roles and 4 platform-level roles control who can do what.
- **HTTP security headers** — Helmet.js protects against XSS, clickjacking, MIME sniffing, and other web attacks.
- **Rate limiting** — 500 requests per 15 minutes globally; 20 requests per 15 minutes on authentication endpoints.
- **CORS policy** — Only the four official frontend origins are allowed to call the API.
- **Immutable audit log** — Every significant action (login, create, update, delete) is logged with the user, IP address, user agent, timestamp, and before/after values. Audit logs cannot be edited or deleted.
- **Data residency** — Production deployment targets Azure South Africa North or Nigerian data centers to comply with the NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation).
